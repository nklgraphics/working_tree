About Me

As a local South Floridian, I've always had an eye for design at a young age. For more than a decade, I've had the privilege to travel and experience many diverse cultures which later influenced my style of work. It made me more aware that quality of a strong design not only impacts the success of any business in its local market, but it can make an impact at a global level.

My strong interest in the visual arts field flourished when I began to study at Broward College. I received multiple recognitions in branding & advertising, photography, typography, user experience & user interface, and web. Not only excelled with individual projects, but enjoyed and did well in a leadership role with team projects facilitating and inspiring others around me to produce amazing projects together, in an effective and timely manner. My true passion is producing modern and clean designs, which is reflected throughout my work as in my personal life. I enjoy getting involved in my community and always seek creative inspirations to innovate my experience as a designer.
